Workflow language

Two finance desks, one evidence-backed path.

AP asks whether a vendor invoice is safe to approve. AR asks what to send when a customer payment is overdue. InvoiceFlow keeps both paths readable: capture the case, extract facts, check policy, recommend the next action, and keep an audit trail.
